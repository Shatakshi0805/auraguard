🔒 Quick Safety Tips
  • Always carry pepper spray or a personal alarm.
  • Add a police siren shortcut sound on your phone.
  • Know Your Rights: You can file a Zero FIR at any police station, no matter where the incident occurred.
  • Share live location with someone you trust.
  • Capture license plates if you feel unsafe.
  • Be aware of your surroundings.
  • Trust your instincts — get out of any situation that feels wrong.
